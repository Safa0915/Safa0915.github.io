Duhaa — Privacy Policy

Effective June 9, 2026

The short version: Duhaa collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no trackers, no servers of ours. Everything you do in Duhaa stays on your device.

What stays on your device

All of it. Your prayer marks, streaks and insights, tasbih counts, Quran bookmarks, settings, and — if you use the Sisters' section — your cycle entries are stored only in the app's local storage on your phone. We have no server and no way to see any of it. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Cycle tracking, specifically

Entries you log in the Sisters' section never leave your device. They are not synced, not backed up to any third party by Duhaa, not shared, and not visible to us or anyone else. They exist solely so the app can gently bridge your streak on excused days.

Location

Prayer times and Qibla direction require your location. The calculation happens entirely on your device using Apple's location services. Duhaa does not transmit your location anywhere. If you prefer, you can skip location access entirely and pick a city by hand.

The only network connections

Duhaa makes no network calls except these:

  • Quran audio — recitation streams from EveryAyah.com when you press play. As with any web server, that service sees the network request (including your IP address). No personal data, prayer data, or identifiers are sent with it.
  • Apple services — standard iOS location/geocoding when you use "Use My Location."

Notifications

Prayer reminders are scheduled locally on your device by iOS. No push servers are involved and nothing about your prayers is transmitted.

Children

Duhaa collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Since the app's design is local-only, we don't expect it to change much.
